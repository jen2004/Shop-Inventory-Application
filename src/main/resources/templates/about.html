Our Story

Green Country Dog Houses was created in 2018 by Amy Miller and her husband John Miller in their garage in Tulsa, Oklahoma. Since then, the company has opened a storefront at 344 S. Yale Ave. and hired 10 employees to serve its human and four-legged customers!

Product Features

  • Our houses can accommodate a dog of ANY size! - We build "Medium" and "Large" size homes, both of which are roomy! Our Medium homes are 40"L x 32"W X 33"H and can accommodate dogs up to 150 pounds. Our Large homes are 48"D x 40"W X 41"H and can accommodate dogs up to 220 pounds.
  • Pet Safe Heaters - All of our houses are equipped with a Chtoocy 600W heater with a thermostat and an anti chew cord.
  • Performance Insulated Liner - All 6 sides of our houses are lined with a 1.0" insulation layer to give your dog 360 degrees of warmth in the cold weather.
  • Weather Resistant and Leak-Proof - All of our houses come with features to keep water and weather out: exterior waterproof paint, elevated floors, and sloped roofs.
  • Durable - Our house frames are constructed of 100% fir wood and metal. Our Medium and Large houses can support a weight of 150 pounds and 220 pounds, respectively.
  • Easy to Clean - The insulation liner cover is removable and machine washable. And the hinged roof makes cleaning a breeze!
  • Your purchase makes a difference! - Green Country Dog Houses donates 10% of all proceeds to the Humane Society of Tulsa
